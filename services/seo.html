Most SEO still optimizes for 2018.

Search changed in 2024-2025. Most agencies didn't. The work shows on a monthly report, not on your bookings.

  • Keyword-stuffed pages: Built for an algorithm that stopped rewarding them years ago.
  • Exact-match backlink chasing: Effort spent on links that no longer move rankings.
  • Vanity reporting: Metrics that climb on a dashboard but never tie to revenue.
  • Blue-links-only thinking: Google's AI Overviews now appear above traditional results for roughly 30% of commercial queries. Buyers research with AI engines first, then verify with Google, then call.

The agencies still optimizing only for the blue links are losing ground. We do both: SEO for Google rankings, AEO for AI engine citations, because that's where attention actually is.

Frequently asked questions

How long does SEO take to show results?+

Meaningful ranking shifts typically take 6-12 months. Most engagements show early signals by month 3-4, with fuller ranking and traffic results landing between months 6 and 12.

What's the difference between SEO and AEO?+

SEO wins clicks on traditional search engines like Google. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) gets you cited inside AI engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity. Both matter now, because buyers search both ways.

What do you include that cheaper SEO retainers don't?+

Ongoing content production, AEO setup, Google Business Profile management, citations, and schema, not just an audit and a few on-page tweaks. Content is the biggest cost in real SEO, and it's where cheap retainers cut corners.

Do you guarantee rankings?+

No. Ranking algorithms aren't public and change constantly, so no one can honestly guarantee rankings. We commit to consistent execution and transparent monthly reporting on what's working and what isn't.

What's included in your AEO work?+

We structure content so AI engines can extract and cite it, implement schema that makes it machine-readable, track where you're cited, and write content built to be the cited answer, not just a blue link.

Who this isn't for.

  • You need leads in the next 30 days. SEO is a 6-to-18-month compounding play.
  • You're not willing to publish content consistently
  • You want guaranteed first-page rankings (no one can honestly promise that)
  • You're looking for the cheapest provider, not the best outcome

We build organic growth that compounds, not quick wins that evaporate at the next algorithm update.
