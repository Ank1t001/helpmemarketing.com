Frequently asked questions.

How long does it take to launch? +

Most builds go live in 2-4 weeks. Simple deployments ship under 2 weeks. Multi-channel builds take 3-4. We give you a concrete date after the discovery call.

What does pricing look like? +

Pricing depends on scope, integrations, and ongoing involvement. We quote per project after we understand your volume, tools, and automation needs. No hidden retainers, no AI licensing markup.

Will the AI sound robotic to my customers? +

Not if it's built right. We train on your team's real conversations, tone, and edge cases. Customers don't notice it's AI. They notice the fast, accurate response.

What happens when the AI can't handle something? +

It escalates to a human with full context. When a request is outside scope, the conversation transfers to the right person with the full transcript. No customer repeats themselves.

How is this different from off-the-shelf chatbots? +

Off-the-shelf chatbots use templates, work in isolation, and break on specifics. We build AI trained on your real conversations, integrated with your tools, and tuned weekly.

Who this isn't for.

  • Businesses with very low customer volume
  • Teams without operational systems or workflows
  • Companies looking to replace humans entirely
  • Anyone expecting a fully custom AI system next week
