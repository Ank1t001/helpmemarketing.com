Questions we get a lot

Do I need to sign a long contract?

No. Our agreements are month-to-month with a 30-day notice. We earn your business every month.

Is ad spend included in the monthly fee?

No. Ad spend is billed directly to your card by Google/Meta — we take zero markup. The retainer covers our management.

How soon will I see results?

Paid ads can drive bookings within 2 weeks. SEO builds over 3-6 months. We set realistic 30/60/90-day milestones in your blueprint.

Do you work with my existing website?

Usually yes. If a rebuild is needed for performance or compliance, we'll flag it in the audit with a fixed price.

What about HIPAA / PHIPA compliance?

Every tracking setup uses HIPAA-compliant tools (Server-side GTM, Meta CAPI with PII stripping). Our compliance lead is a practicing dentist.
