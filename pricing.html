Questions we get a lot

Do I need to sign a long contract?

No. Our agreements are month-to-month with a 30-day notice. We earn your business every month.

Is ad spend included in the monthly fee?

No. Ad spend is billed directly to your card by Google/Meta — we take zero markup. The retainer covers our management.

How soon will I see results?

Paid ads can drive pipeline within 2 weeks. SEO builds over 3–6 months. We set realistic 30/60/90-day milestones in your blueprint.

Do you work with my existing website?

Usually yes. If a rebuild is needed for performance or conversion, we'll flag it in the audit with a fixed-price scope.

Do you handle regulated industries (healthcare, finance)?

Yes. Healthcare clients get HIPAA-aware tracking (server-side GTM, Meta CAPI with PII stripping); finance clients get consent-first, compliance-reviewed creative. Regulated work is scoped into Growth and Enterprise tiers.
