About us

We started this agency because most marketing spend never meets the P&L.

We spent years inside agencies watching good brands buy work that didn't move the number. HelpMeMarketing is the alternative: a small, senior team that treats your P&L like our own. We take fewer clients, ship more per week, and price on outcomes — not billable hours.

Our principles

Four things we won't compromise on.

01

Customer-first, always

We market to real humans making real decisions. No dark patterns, no fake urgency, no manipulative copy.

02

Outcomes over optics

We report on bookings, pipeline, and revenue — not impressions, likes, or reach. Every engagement has a number it has to move.

03

Ethical & compliant

GDPR, CASL, consent-mode, HIPAA where it applies. We know the rules because we live in them — and our creative clears compliance on the first pass.

04

Your team, in sync

We work alongside your founders, marketers, and ops. Marketing that fits the way your team already ships — not the other way around.
