About us

We started this agency because healthcare deserves better marketing.

In 2018, our founders watched a beloved family dentist shut her doors — not because her care was lacking, but because a bigger clinic with a bigger ad budget moved in down the street. We built HelpMeMarketing to level that playing field.

Our principles

Four things we won't compromise on.

01

Patient-first, always

We market to real humans seeking care. No dark patterns, no fake urgency, no scare tactics.

02

Outcomes over optics

We report on booked appointments and revenue — not impressions, likes, or reach.

03

Ethical & compliant

HIPAA-aware tracking. PHIPA, GDPR, CASL. We know the rules because we live in them.

04

Your team, in sync

We work with your front desk, clinicians, and leadership. Marketing that fits your workflow.
